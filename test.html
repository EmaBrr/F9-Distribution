Parrot

Parrot is a leading drone manufacturer, known for innovating professional-grade drones like the ANAFI Ai, the first 4G UAV. Their drones serve various industries, including inspection, firefighting, search-and-rescue, and security, offering robust 4G connectivity for uninterrupted control and data transfer. Parrot also provides open-source software, strong cybersecurity features, and GDPR compliance, ensuring users have control over their data and can easily implement custom solutions.

Ageagle

AgEagle is a leading provider of best-in-class Unmanned Aerial Systems (UAS), sensors and software solutions for customers worldwide in the commercial and government verticals. The company's products include the eBee line of drones designed for mapping and surveillance, as well as the MicaSense series of multispectral and RGB cameras for advanced applications.

Reveal Technology

Reveal is a team of veterans, public safety experts, technologists, and business leaders, focused on delivering advanced AI, computer vision, and edge computing for defense and public safety users. Their flagship product, Farsight, processes sensor data to create real-time 2D maps and 3D models at the tactical edge, all without requiring a network connection. Farsight integrates with military systems, is easy to use, and customizable for different mission needs.

Peli

Peli began in 1976 when scuba enthusiast Dave Parker invented the "Peli Float," a safety tool to help divers. His next product, a durable first-aid case for divers, became popular for protecting valuables, sparking the creation of a company known for high-performance protective cases and lighting systems. Today, Peli is a global leader, trusted by professionals worldwide for over 45 years, with a focus on quality and protecting what matters most.

Vizgard

Vizgard is the UK’s leading provider of platform-agnostic visual AI and precision control solutions for automating real-time camera systems. Their advanced autonomy engine, FortifAI, seamlessly integrates with your existing UxV, C-UAS, and long-range surveillance system, enhancing situational awareness while significantly reducing operator workload. Powered by multiple Deep Learning algorithms, Vizgard’s software delivers time-critical human and vehicle behaviour analysis and accurate geo-location capabilities. It supports plug-and-play human-on-the-loop missions with platforms such as Parrot Anafi USA, CubePilot (MAVLink), and ONVIF Profile S-compatible cameras.
